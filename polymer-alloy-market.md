<p><strong>The Polymer Alloy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PC-based Alloys</li><li>PPO/PPE-based Alloys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymer Alloy Market includes PC-based alloys and PPO/PPE-based alloys. PC-based alloys are made by blending polycarbonate with other polymers to enhance properties such as impact strength and heat resistance. PPO/PPE-based alloys are produced by combining polyphenylene oxide or polyphenylene ether with other polymers to improve chemical resistance and thermal stability. Both types of alloys offer unique characteristics that make them suitable for various applications in industries such as automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
