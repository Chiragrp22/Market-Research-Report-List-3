<p><p>TV antennas can be categorized into two main types: indoor TV antennas and outdoor TV antennas. Indoor TV antennas are designed to be placed inside a building, typically on top of or near a television set. They are usually smaller in size and have a limited range but are easy to install and suitable for urban areas. On the other hand, outdoor TV antennas are larger in size and are mounted outside a building to receive better signal quality and coverage, making them ideal for rural areas or areas with weak signals.</p></p>
