<p><p>Airbag control unit sensors are critical components in automotive safety systems. There are two main types of sensors used in airbag control units: mass type sensors and roller type sensors. Mass type sensors measure the change in mass within the vehicle during a collision, while roller type sensors detect changes in the vehicle's acceleration. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in accurately detecting a collision and deploying the airbag in a timely manner to protect the occupants of the vehicle.</p></p>
