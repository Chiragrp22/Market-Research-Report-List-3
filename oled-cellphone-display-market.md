<p><strong>The OLED Cellphone Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>OLED cellphone displays come in two main types - PMOLED and AMOLED. PMOLED (Passive Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays are simpler and more cost-effective, making them ideal for small screens. On the other hand, AMOLED (Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays offer faster response times, better color reproduction, and higher resolutions, making them popular in high-end smartphones. Both types offer superior picture quality, energy efficiency, and thin design, making them the top choice for many cellphone manufacturers.</p></p>
