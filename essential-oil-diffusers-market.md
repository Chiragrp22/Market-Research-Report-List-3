<p><p>Essential oil diffusers come in various types, each offering unique benefits. Ultrasonic diffusers use water and ultrasonic vibrations to create fine mist, adding moisture to the air. Evaporative diffusers use a fan to evaporate oils quickly, spreading fragrance throughout the room. Nebulizing diffusers break down oils into tiny particles without water or heat, preserving their therapeutic properties. Consumers can choose the type of diffuser based on their preferences for scent intensity, humidity levels, and overall health benefits.</p></p>
