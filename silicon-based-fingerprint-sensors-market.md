<p><strong>The Silicon-Based Fingerprint Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swipe Sensors</li><li>Area Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon-Based Fingerprint Sensors are categorized into two main types - Swipe Sensors and Area Sensors. Swipe Sensors are thin and require the user to swipe their finger across the sensor for authentication. They are commonly found in smartphones and laptops. Area Sensors, on the other hand, are larger and capture the fingerprint image in one touch. They are often used in applications where more security and accuracy are required, such as access control systems and government ID programs.</p></p>
