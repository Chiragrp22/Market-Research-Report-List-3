<p><ul><li>Wheel-On Type</li><li>Direct Drive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart trainers are popular among cyclists for indoor training. There are two main types of smart trainers in the market - Wheel-On Type and Direct Drive Type. Wheel-On Type trainers involve attaching the bike's rear wheel to the trainer, while Direct Drive Type trainers require removing the rear wheel and directly connecting the bike to the trainer. Wheel-On trainers are generally more affordable and easier to set up, while Direct Drive trainers provide a more realistic and immersive riding experience with better accuracy and power transfer.</p></p>
