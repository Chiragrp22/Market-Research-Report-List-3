<p><p>Mobile Peer-To-Peer (P2P) Money Transfer encompasses two main market types: Remote Payment and Proximity Payment. Remote Payment refers to the ability to transfer money between individuals who are not physically present in the same location, using mobile devices. This market type enables seamless transactions across different locations and is commonly used for online purchases or sending money to family and friends. On the other hand, Proximity Payment involves transferring money between individuals who are physically close to each other, typically using near field communication (NFC) technology. This market type facilitates quick and convenient transactions, such as splitting bills or making payments at physical stores.</p></p>
