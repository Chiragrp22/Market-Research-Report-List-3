<p><ul><li>Firmware Over-The-Air Technology (FOTA)</li><li>Software Over-The-Air Technology (SOTA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Over-The-Air (OTA) Update market types include Firmware Over-The-Air Technology (FOTA) and Software Over-The-Air Technology (SOTA). FOTA refers to the process of updating the firmware or embedded software in a vehicle's electronic control units, enhancing its functionality and performance. On the other hand, SOTA involves updating the vehicle's software applications, such as infotainment systems or advanced driver assistance systems, offering new features and security enhancements. Both FOTA and SOTA enable automakers to remotely update vehicles, ensuring optimal performance, enhanced user experience, and increased safety without the need for physical intervention.</p></p>
