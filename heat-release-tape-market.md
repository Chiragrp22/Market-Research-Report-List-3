<p><p>Heat release tape is available in various market types including PET, PVC, PTFE, PE, and PP. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) heat release tape offers excellent heat resistance and insulation properties. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) heat release tape is durable and flexible. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) heat release tape is known for its non-stick and chemical resistance qualities. PE (polyethylene) heat release tape is cost-effective and has good moisture resistance. PP (polypropylene) heat release tape is lightweight and offers high chemical resistance. Each type caters to different application requirements in the market.</p></p>
