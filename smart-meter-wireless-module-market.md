<p><p>The Smart Meter Wireless Module market includes various types such as LoRa and NB-IoT. LoRa (Long Range) technology is known for its low power consumption and long-range communication capabilities, making it ideal for applications like smart meters. NB-IoT (Narrowband Internet of Things) is designed for low-power wide-area networking, allowing smart meters to transmit data efficiently over cellular networks. Both technologies offer different benefits in terms of range, power consumption, and connectivity, catering to different needs in the smart meter market.</p></p>
