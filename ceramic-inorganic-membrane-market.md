<p><p>Ceramic inorganic membranes are used in various applications, with two main types being flat-sheet membranes and pipe membranes. Flat-sheet membranes are thin, flexible sheets that are commonly used in filtration processes for liquids and gases. Pipe membranes, on the other hand, are hollow tubes that are used to separate and purify liquids and gases in industries such as water treatment and pharmaceuticals. Both types of membranes offer high durability, efficiency, and chemical resistance in their respective applications.</p></p>
