<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle SLI Battery market can be divided into three types: Flooded Type Battery, Maintenance-free Type Battery, and Others. Flooded Type Batteries are traditional lead-acid batteries that require periodic maintenance to replenish lost electrolytes. Maintenance-free Type Batteries are sealed lead-acid batteries that do not require refilling of electrolytes, making them more convenient for users. The Others category includes alternative battery technologies such as gel batteries or AGM batteries, offering different features and benefits for commercial vehicle applications.</p></p>
