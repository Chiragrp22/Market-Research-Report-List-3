<p><p>Aluminum Nitride (AlN) Heaters are available in two main types - Flat Heaters and Cylindrical (Tube) Heaters. Flat Heaters provide uniform heat distribution across a flat surface, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent heating of large areas. On the other hand, Cylindrical (Tube) Heaters are designed to provide localized heating within a cylindrical space, making them suitable for applications where heat needs to be focused in a specific area. Both types of heaters are commonly used in industries such as electronics, semiconductors, and aerospace.</p></p>
