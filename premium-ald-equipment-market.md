<p><strong>The Premium ALD Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEALD</li><li>Thermal ALD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Premium ALD Equipment refers to high-quality Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) equipment used in various industries. There are two types: PEALD and Thermal ALD. PEALD, or Plasma-enhanced ALD, utilizes plasma to facilitate deposition, improving film quality and conformality. This technology is preferred for high-density applications like semiconductors and nanotechnology. On the other hand, Thermal ALD relies on thermal reactions to create thin films, making it suitable for materials sensitive to plasma damage, such as organic compounds or sensitive electronic components. Both types play crucial roles in advanced manufacturing processes.</p></p>
