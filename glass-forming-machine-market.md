<p><p>Glass Forming Machine Market Types are categorized into Blow & Blow, Press & Blow, and Narrow Neck Press and Blow (NNPB). Blow & Blow machines are used mainly for the production of wide-mouth containers. Press & Blow machines are suitable for both wide-mouth and narrow-neck containers. NNPB machines are specifically designed for producing narrow-neck containers such as bottles for beverages and pharmaceuticals. Each type caters to different requirements in terms of container size and shape within the glass forming industry.</p></p>
