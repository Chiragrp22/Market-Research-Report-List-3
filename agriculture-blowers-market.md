<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Blowers</li><li>Positive Displacement Blowers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agriculture blowers market consists of two main types: centrifugal blowers and positive displacement blowers. Centrifugal blowers work by using centrifugal force to increase the velocity of air or gas. They are commonly used in applications requiring high air pressure. Positive displacement blowers, on the other hand, operate by trapping air between rotating lobes and a casing, then discharging it at a higher pressure. They are suitable for applications requiring a constant flow of air. Both types play a critical role in various agricultural processes.</p></p>
