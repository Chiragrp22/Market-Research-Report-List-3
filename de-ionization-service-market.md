<p><p>De-Ionization Service Market is segmented into Dual-Beds and Mixed-Beds. Dual-Beds system consists of two separate ion exchange beds, one for cations and one for anions, which help in removing ions from the water. Mixed-Beds system, on the other hand, combines both cations and anions into a single bed, resulting in higher purity water. Both types of markets cater to industries requiring ultra-pure water, such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, and laboratories, where removing all ions from water is crucial.</p></p>
