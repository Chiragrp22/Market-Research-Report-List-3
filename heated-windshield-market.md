<p><strong>The Heated Windshield Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tungsten Wire Heated</li><li>Conductive Layer Heated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heated Windshield market consists of two main types: Tungsten Wire Heated and Conductive Layer Heated. Tungsten Wire Heated windshields use a network of tungsten wires embedded within the glass to generate heat, providing effective defrosting and de-icing capabilities. On the other hand, Conductive Layer Heated windshields have a thin conductive layer applied to the surface of the glass which heats up when an electric current is passed through it. Both types offer efficient and reliable solutions for maintaining visibility in cold weather conditions.</p></p>
