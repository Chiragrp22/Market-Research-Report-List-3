<p><p>Acoustic-insulation rock wool is available in various types to suit different applications. Felty rock wool comes in sheets and is easy to install in walls and ceilings. Strip rock wool is flexible and can be wrapped around pipes or ducts for soundproofing. Tubular rock wool is designed for insulating round or irregularly-shaped structures. Granular rock wool is loose material that can be poured or blown into cavities for insulation. Each type offers unique advantages for effectively reducing sound transmission in buildings.</p></p>
