<p><p>Metastatic Bone Disease Market is segmented into four types:</p><p>1. Medication: This involves the use of drugs to manage symptoms and slow down the progression of the disease. Medications include pain relievers, bone-strengthening drugs, and targeted therapies that inhibit the growth of cancer cells in the bone.</p><p>2. Radiation Therapy: This type of treatment utilizes high-energy radiation to kill cancer cells and alleviate pain. It is commonly used to relieve symptoms and prevent fractures caused by metastatic bone lesions.</p><p>3. Surgical Intervention: In some cases, surgery may be necessary to stabilize bones, remove tumors, or repair fractures. Surgical interventions can help improve mobility and reduce pain for patients with metastatic bone disease.</p><p>4. Tumor Ablation Therapy: This treatment involves using heat (radiofrequency ablation) or extreme cold (cryoablation) to destroy cancer cells in the bone. Tumor ablation therapy is a minimally invasive procedure that can be an alternative to surgery for certain patients.</p></p>
