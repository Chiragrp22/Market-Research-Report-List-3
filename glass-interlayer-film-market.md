<p><p>Glass interlayer film is a material used to laminate glass panes together in order to enhance their strength, durability, and safety. It is typically made of a thin layer of polymer that is sandwiched between two sheets of glass. The film helps to hold the glass together in the event of breakage, reducing the risk of injury from flying glass shards.</p><p>The glass interlayer film market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for safety and security in buildings and automobiles. The growing emphasis on energy efficiency and sustainability in construction projects is also driving the market. Additionally, the rising use of laminated glass in various applications such as facades, automotive glazing, and interior partitions is contributing to the market growth.</p><p>The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.9% during the forecast period.
