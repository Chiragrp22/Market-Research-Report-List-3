<p><ul><li>Physico-Mechanical Methods</li><li>Chemical Methods</li><li>Physico-Chemical Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fragrances microencapsulation refers to the process of enclosing fragrance compounds within microscopic particles. There are three main types of methods used in this market. Physico-Mechanical methods involve the physical coating of the fragrance particles with a protective material. Chemical methods involve the chemical reaction between the fragrance and the protective material to form a coating. Physico-Chemical methods combine both physical and chemical processes to create a protective coating around the fragrance particles. These methods are utilized to enhance the stability and release control of fragrances in various applications.</p></p>
