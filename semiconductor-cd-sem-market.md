<p><strong>The Semiconductor CD SEM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photomask CD-SEM</li><li>Wafer CD-SEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor CD SEM market can be divided into two main types: Photomask CD-SEM and Wafer CD-SEM. Photomask CD-SEM is used for measuring critical dimensions on photomasks, which are essential for semiconductor manufacturing. On the other hand, Wafer CD-SEM is used for measuring critical dimensions on wafers during the semiconductor fabrication process. Both types of CD SEM play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and consistency of semiconductor products, helping manufacturers maintain high standards of production.</p></p>
