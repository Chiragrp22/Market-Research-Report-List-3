<p><p>Household food steamers come in two main types: traditional steamers and electric steamers. Electric steamers, on the other hand, are more convenient as they automate the steaming process with built-in timers and temperature controls. They come in various sizes and designs to cater to different cooking needs. Both types offer a healthier way to cook food by preserving nutrients and flavors.</p></p>
