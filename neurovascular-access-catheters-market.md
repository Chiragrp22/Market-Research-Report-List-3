<p><p>Neurovascular access catheters are medical devices used for removing blood clots, delivering medications, and monitoring blood flow in the brain. These catheters come in three types: single lumen, double lumen, and multiple lumen. Single lumen catheters have only one tube, double lumen catheters have two tubes, and multiple lumen catheters have more than two tubes. The choice of catheter depends on the specific medical procedure and requirements. Single lumen catheters are simpler and used for less complex procedures, while double and multiple lumen catheters offer increased functionality for more complex operations.</p></p>
