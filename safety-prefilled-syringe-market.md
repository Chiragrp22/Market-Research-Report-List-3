<p><p>The safety prefilled syringe market includes two main types: glass-based and plastic-based syringes. Glass-based syringes are traditionally used due to their durability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for drug storage. However, plastic-based syringes have gained popularity in recent years due to their lightweight, ease of disposal, and cost-effectiveness. Both types of syringes prioritize safety features such as needle shields, retractable needles, and auto-disable mechanisms to prevent accidental needle sticks and infection transmission.</p></p>
