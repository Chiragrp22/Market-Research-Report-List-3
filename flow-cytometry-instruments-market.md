<p><ul><li>Cell Analyzers</li><li>Cell Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flow cytometry instruments market can be categorized into two main types: cell analyzers and cell sorters. Cell analyzers are used to analyze and quantify cells based on their size, granularity, and fluorescence properties. They are used in research labs and clinical settings to study various cellular characteristics. Cell sorters, on the other hand, not only analyze cells but also sort them based on their properties. They are advanced instruments capable of isolating specific cell populations for further analysis or applications. Both these instruments play a crucial role in cell analysis and research.</p></p>
