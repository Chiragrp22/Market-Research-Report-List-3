<p><p>There are three main types of customized air motors in the market: vane air motors, piston air motors, and gear motors. Vane air motors use rotating vanes to produce mechanical energy, piston air motors use reciprocating pistons, and gear motors use gears to generate power. Each type of air motor has its own advantages and applications, with vane air motors being compact and lightweight, piston air motors providing high power density, and gear motors offering precise control and high efficiency.</p></p>
