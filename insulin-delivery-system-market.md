Insulin pumps are wearable devices that deliver insulin continuously throughout the day, mimicking the function of a healthy pancreas. Insulin pens are convenient, pre-filled devices that allow for easy and discreet insulin administration. Each type offers unique features and benefits for individuals managing diabetes.</p></p>
