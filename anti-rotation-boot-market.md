<p><p>In the anti-rotation boot market, there are two main types: walking boots and non-walking boots. Walking boots are designed with a sole that allows the wearer to walk with them on, providing support and stability during movement. They are commonly used for post-surgery recovery or injuries that require limited mobility. Non-walking boots, on the other hand, are intended for immobilization purposes and restrict any movement of the foot or ankle. They are typically used for severe fractures or injuries that require complete rest and no weight-bearing activities.</p></p>
