<p><p>Asphalt milling devices come in two main types: wheeled type and tracked type. Wheeled type devices are equipped with tires, making them easier to maneuver on flat surfaces. Tracked type devices have tracks instead of tires, providing better traction and stability on uneven terrain. Both types are commonly used in road construction and maintenance projects to remove old asphalt layers and prepare surfaces for repaving. The choice between wheeled and tracked type devices depends on factors such as the terrain and specific project requirements.</p></p>
