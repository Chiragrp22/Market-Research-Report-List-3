<p><strong>The Solvent Based Paint Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rheology Modification</li><li>Biocides</li><li>Anti-Foaming</li><li>Wetting & Dispersion</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solvent-based paint additives market includes various types such as rheology modification, biocides, anti-foaming agents, wetting & dispersion agents, and others. Rheology modification additives help in improving the flow and leveling properties of the paint. Biocides are used to prevent microbial growth in the paint. Anti-foaming agents reduce the formation of foam during the manufacturing or application process. Wetting & dispersion agents help in achieving uniform distribution of pigments in the paint. Other additives may include anti-settling agents, leveling agents, and drying agents.</p></p>
