<p><p>Mercerised cotton market encompasses various segments such as yarn mercerization, knit mercerization, and cloth mercerization. Yarn mercerization involves treating cotton yarn with caustic soda to increase strength, luster, and dye affinity. Knit mercerization is the process of treating knitted fabrics to create a smooth and shiny finish. Cloth mercerization involves treating woven cotton fabrics to improve their strength, dye uptake, and overall appearance. These different types cater to the diverse needs of the textile industry, offering enhanced quality and properties to cotton products.</p></p>
