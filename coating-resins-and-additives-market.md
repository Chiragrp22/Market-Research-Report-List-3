<p><p>Coating resins are materials used in the production of coatings to enhance their durability, adhesion, and performance. They are classified based on their chemical composition and functionality, such as acrylic, epoxy, and polyurethane resins. Coating additives, on the other hand, are substances added to coatings to improve their properties, such as flow control, leveling, and UV resistance. These additives can include surfactants, rheology modifiers, and crosslinking agents. Both coating resins and additives play a crucial role in achieving the desired quality and performance of coatings.</p></p>
