<p><p>Driving recorders, also known as car dashcams or dash cams, come in two main types: single channel and multi-channel. Single channel dashcams record video footage from the front of the vehicle, while multi-channel dashcams record footage from both the front and rear, and sometimes sides of the vehicle. Single channel dashcams are typically more affordable and easier to install, while multi-channel dashcams provide more comprehensive coverage and protection in the event of an accident or other incident. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
