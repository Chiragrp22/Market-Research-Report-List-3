<p><p>A reversing camera, also known as a backup camera, is a type of video camera that is mounted on the rear of a vehicle to aid the driver in backing up safely. Reversing cameras have become increasingly popular in recent years due to the rising number of accidents caused by blind spots and limited visibility while reversing.</p><p>The Reversing Camera Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.9% during the forecast period.
