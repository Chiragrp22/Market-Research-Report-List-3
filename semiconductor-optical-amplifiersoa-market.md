<p><p>Semiconductor Optical Amplifiers (SOAs) are a type of optical amplifier that use semiconductor materials to amplify optical signals. There are two main types of SOA markets - Erbium Doped Fiber Amplifiers and Linear Optical Amplifiers. Erbium Doped Fiber Amplifiers use erbium-doped fiber to amplify signals in the C and L bands of the optical spectrum, while Linear Optical Amplifiers provide amplification without introducing distortion to the signal. Both types of amplifiers are used in telecommunications networks to boost optical signals for long-distance transmission.</p></p>
