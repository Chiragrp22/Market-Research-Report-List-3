<p><p>Micaceous Iron Oxide (MIO) coatings are available in two major types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based MIO coatings are environmentally friendly, with low VOC content and easy clean-up, making them suitable for indoor applications. On the other hand, solvent-based MIO coatings offer superior durability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for harsh outdoor environments. Both types provide excellent corrosion protection and aesthetic appeal, but the choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
