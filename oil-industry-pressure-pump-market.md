<p><p>The oil industry pressure pump market can be categorized into various types, including centrifugal pumps, mixed flow pumps, axial flow pumps, swirl pumps, and others. </p><p>Centrifugal pumps use rotating impellers to create pressure in the fluid. </p><p>Mixed flow pumps combine characteristics of centrifugal and axial flow pumps. </p><p>Axial flow pumps move fluid parallel to the shaft. </p><p>Swirl pumps create a swirling flow pattern within the pump. </p><p>Other types of pumps in the market may include diaphragm pumps, reciprocating pumps, and gear pumps, among others.</p></p>
