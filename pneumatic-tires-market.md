<p><p>Pneumatic tires can be broadly classified into two market types: solid pneumatics and air pneumatics. Solid pneumatics are tires filled with a solid material, such as foam, providing a puncture-proof solution ideal for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, air pneumatics are traditional tires filled with compressed air, offering a more cushioned ride and better traction on various surfaces. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market, with solid pneumatics being more durable and air pneumatics offering better comfort and performance.</p></p>
