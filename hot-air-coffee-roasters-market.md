<p><ul><li>Solid Drum Roasters</li><li>Perforated Drum Roasters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid Drum Roasters are traditional coffee roasting machines with a solid drum that accumulates heat and evenly roasts the coffee beans. They are best suited for small to medium-scale operations. Perforated Drum Roasters, on the other hand, have small holes in the drum which allow for better airflow and more precise control over the roasting process. They are ideal for larger commercial coffee roasting operations. Both types of roasters offer unique benefits and cater to different market segments based on their capacity and capabilities.</p></p>
