There are two main types of flame spectrometers - UV-Visible spectrometer and IR spectrometer market. UV-Visible spectrometers are used for analyzing samples that absorb in the ultraviolet and visible regions of the electromagnetic spectrum, while IR spectrometers are used for samples that absorb in the infrared region. Both types of spectrometers are commonly employed in various industries for qualitative and quantitative analysis of materials.</p></p>
