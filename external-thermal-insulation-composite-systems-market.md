<p><p>External Thermal Insulation Composite Systems (ETICS) can be classified into two main types: polymer-based and polymer-modified systems. Polymer-based ETICS rely on polymers such as expanded polystyrene for insulation, while polymer-modified systems incorporate polymers like acrylic or silicone for enhanced properties. These systems are widely used in construction for improving thermal performance and energy efficiency of buildings. The choice between polymer-based and polymer-modified systems depends on factors like the building's location, climate, and specific performance requirements.</p></p>
