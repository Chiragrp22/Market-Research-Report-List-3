<p><p>Touch screen stylus market can be classified into active and passive types. Active styluses use a powered tip to register touch input on screens, offering greater precision and additional features like pressure sensitivity. They are often used for professional applications like digital art or note-taking. Passive styluses, on the other hand, rely on a conductive rubber tip to mimic finger touch, making them more affordable and widely available. They are commonly used for general touchscreen interactions on devices like smartphones and tablets.</p></p>
