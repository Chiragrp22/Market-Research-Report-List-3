<p><p>Flying probe test systems are used in the electronics industry to test circuit boards for defects. Single sided probing test systems involve testing one side of the circuit board at a time, while dual sided probing test systems can simultaneously test both sides. Single sided probing test systems are generally more cost-effective and suitable for simpler circuit boards, while dual sided probing test systems are better suited for more complex and advanced circuit boards. Both types of systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of electronic components.</p></p>
