<p><p>Wood-plastic composites are materials made from wood fibers or flour mixed with thermoplastic resins. Polyethylene-based composites are widely used due to their durability and low cost. Polypropylene-based composites offer high stiffness and impact resistance. PVC-based composites are known for their weather resistance and thermal stability. The 'Others' category includes composites made using other types of thermoplastic resins.</p></p>
