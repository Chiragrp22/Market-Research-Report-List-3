<p><ul><li>Etching Process Lead Frame</li><li>Stamping Process Lead Frame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lead frame materials market can be classified into two types based on the manufacturing process: etching process lead frame and stamping process lead frame. Etching process lead frame involves chemically etching the material to create complex shapes with high precision and intricate designs. On the other hand, stamping process lead frame involves pressing the material into shape using a die and stamping process. Both types of lead frame materials offer unique advantages in terms of cost, production efficiency, and design flexibility.</p></p>
