<p><p>The high precision plastic lens market is comprised of two main types: aspheric lens and spherical lens. Aspheric lenses are designed to minimize spherical aberration and distortion, resulting in sharper images and reduced glare. Spherical lenses, on the other hand, have a curved surface with a constant radius, making them simpler to manufacture and less expensive. Both types of lenses are used in a variety of applications such as cameras, microscopes, and projectors, each offering unique benefits for different optical systems.</p></p>
