<p><p>Child resistant packaging refers to packaging designs that are difficult for children to open, reducing the risk of accidental ingestion or exposure to potentially harmful substances. Reclosable packaging allows for repeated opening and closing, providing convenience for consumers while maintaining child-resistant features.
