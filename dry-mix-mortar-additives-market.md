<p><p>Dry mix mortar additives market includes various types such as redispersible polymer powder, plasticizers, defoamers, cellulose ether, air entraining agents, and others. Redispersible polymer powder is used to improve adhesion, flexibility, and workability of mortar. Plasticizers enhance the flow properties of mortar. Defoamers reduce the air content in mortar mixtures. Cellulose ether acts as a thickener and water retention agent. Air entraining agents improve freeze-thaw resistance of mortar. Other additives include accelerators, retarders, and anti-washout agents for specific applications.</p></p>
