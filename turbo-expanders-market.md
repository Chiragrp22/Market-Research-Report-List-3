<p><strong>The Turbo-expanders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Turbo-expander</li><li>Axial Turbo-expander</li><li>Radial-Axial Turbo-expander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Turbo-expanders are used for expanding high-pressure gas to low-pressure gas in various industrial processes. Radial Turbo-expanders feature a radial inflow configuration for efficient gas expansion. Axial Turbo-expanders have an axial inflow design, making them suitable for high flow rates. Radial-Axial Turbo-expanders combine features of both radial and axial designs, offering a balance of efficiency and flow capacity. Each type of turbo-expander serves specific applications based on factors like gas flow rate, pressure levels, and efficiency requirements in industries such as oil & gas, power generation, and chemical processing.</p></p>
