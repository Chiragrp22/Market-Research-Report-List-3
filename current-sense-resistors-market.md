<p><p>Current sense resistors come in two main market types - SMD Type and Through Hole Type. SMD Type resistors are surface mount devices that are smaller in size, making them suitable for high-density circuit board designs. They are also known for their precision and accuracy in measuring current. On the other hand, Through Hole Type resistors are larger in size and are installed by inserting their leads into holes on the circuit board. They are commonly used in applications where reliability and durability are key factors.</p></p>
