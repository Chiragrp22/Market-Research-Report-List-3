<p><p>Hurricane resistant glass is available in two main types: laminated glass and insulated laminated glass. Laminated glass consists of two or more panes of glass bonded together with a layer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) in between, providing strength and durability. Insulated laminated glass includes an additional layer of insulation between the glass panes, improving energy efficiency and sound insulation. Both types of glass are designed to withstand high winds and impact, making them ideal for hurricane-prone areas.</p></p>
