<p><strong>The Spectrum Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Tuned</li><li>Vector Signal</li><li>Real-Time</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swept-Tuned Spectrum Analyzers operate by scanning a predetermined frequency range sequentially, making them suitable for measuring continuous wave signals. Vector Signal Analyzers, on the other hand, are designed to handle complex modulated signals with high accuracy in terms of amplitude and phase. Real-Time Spectrum Analyzers process signals instantly, offering the ability to capture and analyze dynamic and transient events in real time. Each type caters to different technological needs and applications within the spectrum analyzer market.</p></p>
