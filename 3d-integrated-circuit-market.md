<p><ul><li>Through Silicon Via</li><li>Silicon Interposer</li><li>Through Glass Via</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D Integrated Circuit Market Types incorporate Through Silicon Via (TSV), Silicon Interposer, and Through Glass Via technologies. TSV involves stacking multiple layers of silicon wafers using vertical connections, offering high performance and reduced footprint. Silicon Interposer technology utilizes a silicon wafer to connect multiple components horizontally, enabling faster communication and power efficiency. Through Glass Via technology involves using glass substrates to connect different layers, providing improved thermal conductivity and miniaturization. These market types cater to various industry needs for advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
