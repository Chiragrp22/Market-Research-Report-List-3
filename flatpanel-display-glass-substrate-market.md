<p><p>The flat panel display glass substrate market is divided into three types: 2D glass, 2.5D glass, and 3D glass. 2D glass refers to a traditional flat glass used for displays, such as LCDs. 2.5D glass involves a slight curvature to enhance aesthetics while maintaining flatness. It is typically used for smartphones and wearables. In contrast, 3D glass has a significantly curved surface, providing a more immersive viewing experience.
