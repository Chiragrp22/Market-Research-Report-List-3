<p><p>HMI Consoles come in two main types: Fixed Type and Mobile Type. Fixed Type refers to consoles that are permanently installed in a specific location, providing a stable interface for operators to control machinery and equipment. These consoles are ideal for applications where operators have designated workstations. On the other hand, Mobile Type refers to consoles that can be easily moved around the workspace, allowing operators to control machinery from different locations. This flexibility is beneficial for industries with dynamic or changing operational needs.</p></p>
