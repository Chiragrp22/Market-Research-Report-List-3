<p><ul><li>Composite Membranes</li><li>Asymmetric Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Seawater desalination reverse osmosis membrane market types include composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes are made up of multiple layers that provide increased efficiency and performance in removing impurities from seawater. Asymmetric membranes have a dense top layer and a porous bottom layer, allowing for higher flux rates and better salt rejection. Both types of membranes are essential in the seawater desalination process, offering varying benefits and capabilities to meet the specific needs of different desalination plants.</p></p>
