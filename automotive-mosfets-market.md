<p><strong>The Automotive MOSFETs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>N-Channel</li><li>P-Channel</li><li>Dual N and P-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive MOSFETs are categorized into different types based on their channel configurations. N-Channel MOSFETs are widely used due to their high efficiency and fast switching speeds. P-Channel MOSFETs are used for low-side switching applications. Dual N and P-Channel MOSFETs offer the advantages of both N and P-Channel devices in a single package, providing designers with flexibility in designing automotive applications. Each type of MOSFET has its own unique characteristics and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
