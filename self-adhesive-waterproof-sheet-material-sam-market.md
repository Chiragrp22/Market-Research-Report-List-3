<p><p>Self-adhesive waterproof sheet material (SAM) is available in various types, including double-sided, wet installed cross-laminated, non-filler base, and others. Double-sided SAMs have adhesive on both sides for easy installation. Wet installed cross-laminated SAMs are layers of material that are laminated together and installed using a wet method. Non-filler base SAMs do not contain any fillers, making them more durable. Other types of SAMs may include specific features or materials tailored to different applications.</p></p>
