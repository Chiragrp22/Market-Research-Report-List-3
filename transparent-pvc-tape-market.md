<p><p>Transparent PVC tape is available in two main types - One-Sided and Two-Sided. One-Sided tape has adhesive on only one side, making it suitable for applications where only one surface needs to be protected or bound. On the other hand, Two-Sided tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing for a stronger bond between two surfaces. Both types are commonly used in packaging, sealing, and crafting applications, offering versatility and reliability in various tasks.</p></p>
