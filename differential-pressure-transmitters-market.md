<p><p>Differential pressure transmitters are categorized into two main types: Digital Type and Analog Type. Digital Type transmitters utilize digital technology to provide accurate and reliable pressure measurements. They offer improved accuracy and stability compared to Analog Type transmitters, making them suitable for high precision applications. Analog Type transmitters, on the other hand, use analog technology to provide pressure readings. They are known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, but may not offer the same level of accuracy as Digital Type transmitters. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
