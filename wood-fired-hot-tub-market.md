<p><p>The wood-fired hot tub market can be segmented into two types based on the heater placement: internal heater and external heater. Internal heaters are built inside the hot tub and are typically more compact, efficient, and provide faster heating. External heaters, on the other hand, are separate units placed outside the tub and usually take longer to heat the water but offer greater flexibility in terms of maintenance and repair. Customers can choose between these options based on their preferences for heating speed, space availability, and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
