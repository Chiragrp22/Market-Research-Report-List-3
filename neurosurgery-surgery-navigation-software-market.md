<p><p>The Neurosurgery Surgery Navigation Software market consists of various types of navigation systems used during surgery. Electromagnetic Navigation Systems utilize electromagnetic fields to track instruments and provide real-time guidance. Hybrid Navigation Systems combine different technologies, such as electromagnetic and optical, for improved accuracy. Optical Navigation Systems utilize cameras and infrared markers to track instruments. Fluoroscopy-Based Navigation Systems use X-rays to guide surgical instruments. CT-Based Navigation Systems use images from CT scans for precise guidance. Lastly, there are other navigation systems that utilize different technologies or a combination of existing ones.</p></p>
