<p><strong>The Needle Free Extension Set Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Lumen (SL)</li><li>Double-Lumen (DL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Needle-Free Extension Sets are available in two market types: Single-Lumen (SL) and Double-Lumen (DL). Single-Lumen sets have one channel for fluid transfer, making them suitable for simple infusions. Double-Lumen sets, on the other hand, have two separate channels for simultaneous administration of different medications or fluids. This makes them ideal for more complex treatments or situations where multiple medications need to be delivered at the same time. Both Single-Lumen and Double-Lumen sets offer the convenience and safety of needle-free connections for healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
