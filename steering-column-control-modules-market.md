There are two main types of steering column control modules based on their placement: on the steering wheel and around/below the steering wheel. On the steering wheel modules typically include buttons or toggles for functions like audio controls and cruise control. Around/below the steering wheel modules may consist of stalks or levers for adjusting lights, wipers, and other vehicle functions.</p></p>
