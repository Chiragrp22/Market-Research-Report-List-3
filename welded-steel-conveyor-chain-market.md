<p><p>Welded steel conveyor chain types Type W and Type WD are commonly used in industrial settings for heavy-duty conveying applications. Type W chains have a larger base, providing better wear resistance and higher strength capacities, making them suitable for demanding environments. On the other hand, Type WD chains feature a double pitch design, allowing for shorter attachments and wider link plates, enabling efficient conveying of heavier loads with reduced chain weight. Both types offer durability, reliability, and high performance in various conveyor systems.</p></p>
