<p><p>Fibre Reinforced Plastic (FRP) is a composite material made of a polymer matrix reinforced with fibres. Two main types of FRP markets are PAN-Based and Pitch-Based. PAN-Based FRP is made from polyacrylonitrile fibres, offering high strength and stiffness. On the other hand, Pitch-Based FRP is made from carbon fibres derived from coal tar or petroleum pitch, providing excellent thermal conductivity and resistance to chemicals. Both types of FRP materials have distinct properties that cater to different applications in various industries.</p></p>
