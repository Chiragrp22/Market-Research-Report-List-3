<p><p>Paint rheological additives market can be divided into two main types: inorganic type and organic type. Inorganic type additives are derived from minerals and metals, such as clays and silica, and are used to improve flow control and stability in paints. On the other hand, organic type additives are derived from natural or synthetic chemicals and are used to regulate viscosity and thixotropy in paints. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and quality of paints in different applications.</p></p>
