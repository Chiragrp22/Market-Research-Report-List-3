<p><p>An air handling unit (AHU) is a device used to control and circulate air within a building or facility. AHUs are essential in maintaining indoor air quality, controlling temperature and humidity, and overall comfort in commercial, residential, and industrial buildings.</p><p>The global air handling unit market has been experiencing significant growth over the past few years and is expected to continue this trend during the forecast period. The integration of sensors, advanced control systems, and cloud computing enables remote monitoring and control of air handling units, optimizing their performance and energy consumption.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the use of sustainable and eco-friendly materials in AHUs, as there is a growing focus on reducing the environmental impact of HVAC systems.
