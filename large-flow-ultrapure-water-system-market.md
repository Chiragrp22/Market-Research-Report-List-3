<p><p>Large flow ultrapure water systems are essential for industries requiring ultra-high purity water, such as semiconductor manufacturing and pharmaceuticals. Two common types are Reverse Osmosis+Ion Exchange and Reverse Osmosis+EDI. Reverse Osmosis removes ions and contaminants from water, while Ion Exchange further purifies it by exchanging ions. Reverse Osmosis+EDI combines Reverse Osmosis with Electrodeionization, a process that uses electricity to remove ions from water. Both systems are highly effective in producing ultrapure water for critical industrial applications.</p></p>
