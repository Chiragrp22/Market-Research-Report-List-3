Cellulases, on the other hand, can degrade cellulose, a major component of plant cell walls, into fermentable sugars. These enzymes play a crucial role in the production of biofuels from renewable feedstocks. Other enzymes in this market may include proteases, lipases, and ligninases, which contribute to the breakdown of proteins, lipids, and lignin present in biomass for biofuel production.</p></p>
