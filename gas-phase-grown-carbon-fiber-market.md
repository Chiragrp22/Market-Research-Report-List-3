PAN Based carbon fiber is produced from polyacrylonitrile and is known for its high strength, stiffness, and heat resistance. On the other hand, Asphalt Based carbon fiber is derived from petroleum pitch and offers excellent thermal conductivity and chemical resistance. Both types of carbon fiber find applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods due to their unique properties and lightweight nature.</p></p>
