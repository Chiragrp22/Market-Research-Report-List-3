<p><strong>The Medical MRI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnet System</li><li>RF System</li><li>Computer Imaging System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical MRI equipment market includes three main types of systems: magnet system, RF system, and computer imaging system. The magnet system is responsible for generating a powerful magnetic field to align the protons in the patient's body. The RF system emits radio frequency pulses that manipulate the protons in the patient's body, allowing their detection and imaging. Lastly, the computer imaging system processes the data received from the magnet and RF systems to create detailed images of the patient's anatomy for diagnosis and treatment planning purposes.</p></p>
