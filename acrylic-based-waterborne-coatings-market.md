<p><p>Acrylic based waterborne coatings are divided into two main types: liquid and powder. Liquid coatings are applied as a liquid solution and are typically used for larger surface areas or complex shapes. Powder coatings, on the other hand, are applied as a dry powder and are cured through a heat process. They are often used for smaller or more intricately designed parts. Both types of coatings offer excellent corrosion resistance, durability, and environmental benefits compared to traditional solvent-based coatings.</p></p>
