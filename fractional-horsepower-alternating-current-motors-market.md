<p><p>Fractional horsepower alternating current motors are commonly categorized into three main types: Split Phase Motors, Shaded Pole Motors, and Reluctance Motors. </p><p>Split Phase Motors have a starting winding and a running winding to provide the necessary torque to start the motor.</p><p>Shaded Pole Motors have a simple design with a shaded pole on the stator to create a rotating magnetic field.</p><p>Reluctance Motors work on the principle of the rotor trying to align itself with the stator magnetic field.</p></p>
