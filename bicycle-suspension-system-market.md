<p><p>The bicycle suspension system market can be divided into two main types: Hardtail Suspension and Full Suspension. </p><p>Hardtail Suspension bikes have a suspension fork in the front wheel only, providing a smoother ride over rough terrain but with more efficient pedaling. </p><p>Full Suspension bikes have suspension systems in both the front and rear wheels, offering even greater shock absorption and comfort on bumpy trails, but may sacrifice some pedaling efficiency. </p><p>Both types cater to different riding preferences and terrain conditions.</p></p>
