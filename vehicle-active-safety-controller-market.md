<p><p>Vehicle Active Safety Controller market consists of two main types: Domain Controller and Distributed Controller. Domain Controller market focuses on centralized control units that manage specific functions within the vehicle, such as braking or steering. On the other hand, Distributed Controller market involves separate control units distributed throughout the vehicle, each responsible for different safety functions. Both types aim to enhance vehicle safety by integrating various technologies and systems to prevent accidents and protect both the vehicle and its occupants.</p></p>
