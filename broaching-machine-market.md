<p><p>Broaching machines are categorized into two main types: Vertical Broaching Machine and Horizontal Broaching Machine. Vertical broaching machines are designed for pulling the broach tool vertically through the workpiece, while horizontal broaching machines are used for pushing the broach tool horizontally through the workpiece. Both types of machines are used for precision cutting and shaping of materials, but the orientation of the broaching process differs between the two types. Each type has its own unique applications and benefits in various industries.</p></p>
