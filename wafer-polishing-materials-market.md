<p><p>The wafer polishing materials market includes various types such as CMP slurry, CMP pads, post CMP cleaning solution, and others. CMP slurry is used for chemical-mechanical polishing of wafers, while CMP pads provide the mechanical abrasion during the polishing process. Post CMP cleaning solution is used to remove residues left after polishing. Other materials may include polishing pads, polishing compounds, and slurries for specific applications. These materials are essential for ensuring the precision and quality of wafer polishing in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
