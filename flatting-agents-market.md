<p><p>Flatting agents refer to additives used in coatings and paints to reduce gloss and provide a matte finish. The market types for flatting agents include silica, waxes, thermoplastics, and others. Silica flatting agents are popular for their high efficiency and transparency. Waxes are known for their excellent abrasion resistance and slip properties. Thermoplastic flatting agents offer good chemical resistance and compatibility with various resin systems. Other flatting agents may include polymers, silica gels, and metal oxides, each with unique properties and applications.</p></p>
