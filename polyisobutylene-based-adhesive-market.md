<p><p>Polyisobutylene based adhesives have two main types: water soluble adhesive and hot melt adhesive. Water soluble adhesive is a type of adhesive that can be dissolved in water, making it easy to clean up and environmentally friendly. On the other hand, hot melt adhesive is a type of adhesive that is applied in its molten state and solidifies upon cooling. It is commonly used in applications where a strong and fast bond is required, such as in packaging and woodworking.</p></p>
