<p><p>The Stainless Steel Orthodontic Wire Market includes two types of wires: Multi-Strand Stainless Steel Wire and Single Strand Stainless Steel Wire. Multi-Strand Stainless Steel Wire is made up of multiple thin stainless steel wires twisted together to form a stronger wire. It provides better flexibility and adjustment options for orthodontic treatments. On the other hand, Single Strand Stainless Steel Wire is a single thick wire used for simpler orthodontic cases. It offers less flexibility but is still effective in dental alignment.</p></p>
