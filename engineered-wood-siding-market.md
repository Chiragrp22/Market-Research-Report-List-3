<p><strong>The Engineered Wood Siding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oriented Strand Board</li><li>High-density Fiberboard</li><li>Veneered Plywood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineered wood siding comes in various types including Oriented Strand Board (OSB), High-density Fiberboard, and Veneered Plywood. OSB is a cost-effective option made from layers of wood strands bonded together, while High-density Fiberboard is a dense and durable option often used for its moisture resistance. Veneered Plywood is made by layering thin sheets of wood veneer over a plywood base, offering a decorative finish. Each type of engineered wood siding offers unique benefits in terms of cost, durability, and aesthetics.</p></p>
