<p><strong>The Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption-type</li><li>Reflection-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Absorption-type goggles are designed to absorb and filter specific wavelengths of light, providing protection for the eyes by reducing the intensity of light entering the eyes. These goggles are commonly used in laboratories and industrial settings where there is a risk of exposure to harmful light sources. Reflection-type goggles, on the other hand, work by reflecting light away from the eyes, preventing direct exposure to bright lights. These goggles are often used in sports and outdoor activities where glare from the sun can be distracting or harmful to vision.</p></p>
