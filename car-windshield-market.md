<p><p>The car windshield market includes two main types based on the material used: thermoplastic and thermoset. Thermoplastic windshields are made of materials that soften when heated and harden when cooled, allowing for easy reshaping. Thermoset windshields, on the other hand, are made of materials that undergo a permanent chemical change when heated, making them more heat and chemical resistant. Both types of materials offer unique advantages and are used in different applications within the automotive industry.</p></p>
