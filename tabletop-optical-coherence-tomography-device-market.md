<p><strong>The Tabletop Optical Coherence Tomography Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spectral-Domain OCT</li><li>Swept-Source OCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tabletop Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) devices come in two main types: Spectral-Domain OCT and Swept-Source OCT. Spectral-Domain OCT uses a broadband light source to provide high imaging speeds and resolution, making it ideal for clinical applications. Swept-Source OCT, on the other hand, uses a tunable laser source to achieve even higher imaging speeds and deeper tissue penetration, making it suitable for research and more advanced clinical applications. Both types of OCT devices are compact and convenient for use on a tabletop setting.</p></p>
