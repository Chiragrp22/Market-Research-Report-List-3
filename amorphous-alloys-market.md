<p><p>Amorphous alloys, also known as metallic glasses, can be categorized into two main types based on their structure: ordered and disordered. Ordered structure amorphous alloys have a defined arrangement of atoms, providing them with specific properties and characteristics. On the other hand, disordered structure amorphous alloys lack a specific atomic arrangement, resulting in a more random structure with different properties. Both types of amorphous alloys have unique applications and advantages in various industries.</p></p>
