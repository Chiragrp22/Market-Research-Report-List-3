<p><p>Diffractive elements are used in various applications such as beam shaping (Top-Hat), beam splitting, and beam focusing. Beam shaping elements help in converting a Gaussian beam into a uniform intensity distribution. Beam splitting elements divide a single beam into multiple beams for parallel processing. Beam focusing elements help in concentrating light to a specific focal point. Each market type caters to different requirements in industries such as laser processing, telecommunications, and imaging, providing solutions for various optical applications.</p></p>
