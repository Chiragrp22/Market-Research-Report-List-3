Flush Type machines use deionized water to continuously flush away debris. Submerged Type machines have the workpiece submerged in dielectric fluids throughout the machining process. Other types of Wire EDM machines may include those with additional features or variations in design. Each type offers unique advantages and capabilities, making them suitable for different applications and industries within the manufacturing sector.</p></p>
