<p><p>Fall protection equipment and system market includes various types of safety gear such as harnesses, lanyards, self-retracting lifelines, belts, and others. Harnesses are worn around the torso and thighs to prevent falls. Lanyards are used to connect the harness to a secure anchor point. Self-retracting lifelines automatically stop a fall and reduce the impact on the body. Belts provide additional support and stability. Other types of fall protection equipment include anchor points, guardrails, and safety nets.</p></p>
