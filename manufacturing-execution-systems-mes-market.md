<p><p>Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) are software solutions that help manage and control manufacturing operations on the shop floor. In terms of market types, there are three main categories: on-premises, on-demand, and hybrid. On-premises MES refers to software installed and operated on a company's own servers and infrastructure. On-demand MES, on the other hand, is cloud-based and accessed through the internet, allowing for scalability and easy access. Hybrid MES combines elements of both on-premises and on-demand, leveraging the advantages of both approaches for more flexibility and customization.</p></p>
