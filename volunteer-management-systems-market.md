<p><p>Volunteer Management Systems (VMS) are software platforms designed to streamline and enhance the management of volunteers within organizations. The market can be categorized into two types: Cloud Based and On-Premises. Cloud-based VMS refers to systems hosted on remote servers, accessible through the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability and cost-effectiveness but require internet connectivity. On the other hand, On-Premises VMS are installed and run on local servers within an organization's infrastructure. They provide greater control, data security, and customizability but require dedicated hardware and IT support.</p></p>
