<p><p>High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) Devices are categorized into MR-Guided and Ultrasound-Guided markets based on the imaging technology used during the procedure. MR-Guided HIFU devices utilize magnetic resonance imaging to provide real-time visualization of the targeted tissue, allowing for precise treatment. Ultrasound-Guided HIFU devices use ultrasound imaging for guidance during the procedure. Both types of devices offer non-invasive treatment options for various medical conditions, such as cancer and other diseases, with minimal side effects and shorter recovery times.</p></p>
