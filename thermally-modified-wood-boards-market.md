<p><strong>The Thermally Modified Wood Boards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermo-S</li><li>Thermo-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermally Modified Wood Boards are categorized into two main types: Thermo-S and Thermo-D. Thermo-S boards undergo the thermal modification process on only one side, resulting in a material that is stable and resistant to decay. Thermo-D boards, on the other hand, are thermally modified on both sides, making them even more durable and suitable for outdoor use. Both types of boards are popular in the construction industry for their enhanced properties such as increased stability, improved moisture resistance, and enhanced natural appearance.</p></p>
