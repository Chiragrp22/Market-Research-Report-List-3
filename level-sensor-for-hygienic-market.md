<p><p>Level sensors for the hygienic market are essential for ensuring accurate measurement of liquid levels in various applications. Two common types are contact and non-contact sensors. Contact sensors come in direct contact with the liquid to provide accurate readings, making them suitable for food and pharmaceutical industries. Non-contact sensors use technologies like ultrasonic or laser to measure levels without touching the liquid, making them ideal for applications where contamination must be prevented. Both types offer reliable and accurate level measurement solutions for the hygienic market.</p></p>
