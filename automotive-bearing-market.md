<p><p>The automotive bearing market can be segmented into three main types: ball bearings, roller bearings, and others. Ball bearings are known for their ability to reduce friction and support radial and axial loads. Roller bearings, on the other hand, use cylindrical rollers to support heavier loads and handle higher speeds. The "others" category includes special types of bearings such as angular contact bearings, tapered roller bearings, and needle roller bearings, each designed for specific automotive applications.</p></p>
